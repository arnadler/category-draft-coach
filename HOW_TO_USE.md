# Category Draft Coach - Quick Start Guide

**Website:** https://category-draft-coach.vercel.app

This tool helps you make smarter picks during your fantasy baseball draft. It tracks your team and tells you which players will help you the most in each scoring category.

---

## Before Your Draft

### 1. Set Up Your League Settings

Click the **Settings** button (gear icon) in the top right corner.

- **Number of Teams:** Set this to match your league (usually 10 or 12)
- **Categories:** Make sure the scoring categories match your league (R, HR, RBI, SB, AVG, W, SV, K, ERA, WHIP, etc.)
- **Roster Slots:** Adjust if your league has different roster sizes

Click **Save** when done.

---

## During Your Draft

### Adding Players You Draft

When you draft a player:

1. **Quick Add (Easiest):** Type the player's name in the search bar at the top and press Enter
2. **Or Browse:** Scroll through the player list on the left side and click on a player, then click "My Pick"

### Marking Players Other Teams Draft

When someone else drafts a player:

1. Find the player in the list on the left
2. Click on them
3. Click "Other Team" - this removes them from the available pool without adding them to your roster

### Understanding the Screen

The screen has three main sections:

| Left Side | Middle | Right Side |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| Available Players | Your Roster + Category Scores | Recommended Picks |

### Reading Your Category Scores (Middle Section)

- **Green bars** = You're doing well in that category
- **Red bars** = You need help in that category
- The app automatically weighs categories where you're behind more heavily

### Following the Recommendations (Right Side)

The app shows you the **Top 5 Best Picks** based on your current roster. Each recommendation shows:

- The player's name and position
- How much they'll help your overall team score
- Which specific categories they help (like "+0.45 SB" or "+0.22 ERA")

**Tip:** You don't have to follow the recommendations exactly - they're suggestions to help you make informed decisions!

---

## Helpful Tips

1. **Don't worry about saving** - Your draft progress is automatically saved in your browser. You can close the tab and come back later.

2. **Balance is key** - The app helps you build a balanced roster rather than being great at some categories and terrible at others.

3. **Position scarcity matters** - Catchers and shortstops are harder to find, so the app gives them a small boost.

4. **Use on a laptop/computer** - The site works best on a larger screen during your draft.

---

## Starting Fresh

Want to start over? Click **Settings** and look for a **Reset** or **Clear Draft** option, or simply clear your browser data for the site.

---

## Need Help?

If something isn't working, try refreshing the page. Your draft progress will still be there!
